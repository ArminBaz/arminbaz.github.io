About Me
Motha Fucka

I am currently finishing my undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering at USC and I will be entering the Masters program in Electrical Engineering this summer (2020). Funnily enough the masters program is going to concentrate in data science and machine learning, so I won't really be using most of undergraduate knowledge. Was it a waste? Sort of. However, I do believe that studying Electrical Engineering gave me a solid foundation for many different fields and it opened my eyes to a lot of areas of technology that I was previously unaware of. Who knows, if I didn't study EE, I might not even be typing this meaningless about me right now.

So what the fuck are you interested in? Many, many things, so many things. I consider myself more of a renaissance man, except instead of being good at everything, I am slightly mediocre at everything. I really enjoy machine learning, particularly deep learning (go figure), philsophy, and cognitive sciences. I say cognitive sciences because that sounds more all encompassing, which is right up my alley of surface level knowledge.
