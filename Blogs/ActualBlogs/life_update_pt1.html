Life Update pt. 1

It has been almost exactly a month (+1 day) since my last blog post, so I thought I should give an update.

Things are going really well, thats it. Seriously, it's been a while since things have been this good. I finally came to terms with the fact that I want to torture myself with the pursuit of PhD, I found a topic that I am geniunely passionate about, I am taking a class that really interests me, and I feel like I have deepened my relationships despite being in the tail end of a pandemic (should probably make a blog post update that soon too).

I really can't complain about anything. I feel like I spent the better part of the last three years just dealing with random stresses that I placed on myself. So, now that I really don't have that... it feels good. Go figure. I guess, as the saying goes, the older you get the more you tend to realize that all the bullshit of life will always just stick with you. It's really up to you to navigate your way through it and to extract the real meaning from between the thorns. Not to say life is one big shitshow, but sometimes it really can be. At the end of the day it really is what you make of it.

So, if there's anything that I truly learned over the last few years its to seriously stop stressing about the little things. This is advice that has been drilled into us from since any of us can remember. But, it really takes consistent and conscious effort to be able to translate these words into a lifestyle. I guess that's the biggest problem with these "philosophies on life", whenever we hear it we all think to ourselves "Of course! Why would I want to live my life in any other way?". Most often, that's just it. Just words on how others want to try and live their lives. It is up to the individual to digest the word and parse the meaning out of it in a way that only they can understand. We may all be human, but our rich and unique conscious experiences of life make us all incredibely different.
